Who We Are

A Forward-Thinking Team of Builders

Neria Solutions is a forward-thinking company dedicated to transforming scientific theories into practical, real-world applications. Our team of passionate scientists, developers, and educators is committed to solving global challenges through innovation and collaboration.

Based in Bloemfontein, South Africa, we believe that technology should serve communities — especially those who need it most. From agriculture to education to renewable energy, we build solutions with purpose.
